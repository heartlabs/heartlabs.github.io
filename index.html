About Us

Interpreting songs by Django Reinhardt, Leonard Cohen, João Gilberto and more. Every performance is a unique surprise for us like for you when we capture the present mood with our largely improvised interpretations.

Neidhart Orlich - Violin
Stefan Raschbacher - Guitar, Vocals

Two happy musicians from eastern Austria, playing together since the summer of '18.

We play for all audiences, in all locations, all kind of collaborations and at every possible kind of events. Nothing is too mundane, nothing too mad for us.

We played at festivals, vernissages, a holy mass, living rooms, parks, streets, parties, charity events, weddings and poem nights.

We play for the old and the young, the wise and the insane, the happy and the hurt, the rich and the poor, the hungry and the satisfied, the restless and the rooted.
